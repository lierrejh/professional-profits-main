FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions
Are zoom meetings often?

We hold zoom meetings once a week for market recap / planning for the week ahead, but occasionally hold midweek meetings too for 1:1's and bootcamps.

What level of experience with trading do I need to have?

We accomodate to the needs of the individual. If a trader understands the market and just wants to learn the strategy, then they can. Same goes for if they are a complete beginner, with extra emphasis on building the knowledge needed in the market.

What times are the meeetings hosted?

Daily market chat begins at ~12pm GMT, ready for the New York Open. Weekend meetings begin on a Sunday at 8pm.

Will the Live Sessions/Classes be recorded?

Yes, all of the live sessions will be recorded for you to keep and revisit - along with descriptions provided if you would like to read.

How does the joining process work?

Due to limited spaces, when registering you will join the waitlist. Once a spot opens, you will be contacted and able to join the mentorship.

Am I obliged to join if I am accepted on the waitlist.

You do not have to join if you no longer feel that you want to. Upon being invited to join, you will have a week to accept or deny youe space, if we do not hear back, then we will take it as you have denied. Your spot will be then given to someone else. If you change your mind, you will have to rejoin the waitlist.

Do I require my own capital to trade with?

We trade using prop-firms, which is the best method for most. If you have your own capital then you are welcome to trade it on your own personal account.

What happens if I do not see immediate improvement?

Trading is not quick overnight money. Trading is a business and so you must treat it like one. Many members can confirm that results come over time, and you will improve with more effort and time.
